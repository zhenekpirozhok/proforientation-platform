# Career Guidance Platform Based on Personality Tests and AI-Powered Profession Matching

This project is an intelligent, user-friendly career orientation platform designed to help students and young professionals discover career paths that align with their interests, skills, and personality traits. 
Through interactive quizzes based on psychological profiling and data-driven analysis, the platform provides personalized career recommendations and development insights. 
It addresses the common challenge of uncertainty in career decision-making by transforming self-assessment results into clear, actionable guidance. 
What makes the platform unique is its combination of structured assessment, localization support, and adaptive analytics, enabling users to make informed career choices with confidence
